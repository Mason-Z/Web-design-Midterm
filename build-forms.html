Knight system
Original: ライダーシステム / Rider System
The main key in the creation plan is also the transformation device of the knight. The designer is Gecheng Ren, and the developer is Ge Chengqiao. The purpose of the development was to create the strongest defense system (Kamen Rider), but it was intended by the officials of the Sandu government to use this as a national military use. The system can be used when the subject's hazard level is above 3.0 or above. Later, it was learned from the Tongsheng war rabbit mouth that recovered the memory of Ge Chengqiao that the knight system was developed to fight against Abruth.

Structure
Original: ハーフボディ / Half Body
The body structure of the Kamen Rider Build, similar to the left and right half of the Kamen Rider W, is generated by creating a drive with a full bottle, and the different combinations will correspond to the various forms of Kamen Rider Build. The armor consists of the "organic matter" and "inorganic" properties in the chemical experiments corresponding to the respective full bottles. The organic property armor covers the left side of the oblique line of the head and the right side of the oblique line of the trunk. And the left and left feet, while the inorganic attribute armor covers the area outside the waist.

Original: ハザードボディ / Hazard Body
The body structure of the Kamen Rider builds a dangerous form by two structures that are equivalent to a half-body. It is generated by the creation of a dangerous trigger and a full bottle. The whole body is dark but the compound eyes are the same as the corresponding Best Match form, with amazing power.
